About Restoration Ministry in Ukraine

RESTORATION MINISTRY IN UKRAINE INCORPORATED is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law for charitable purposes. We are a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit with EIN 82-1387545.

Specific purpose: to assist charitable organizations in Ukraine who work with poor, disabled, and foster children.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and humanitarian purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
