RESTORATION MINISTRY IN UKRAINE INCORPORATED

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our specific purpose is to assist charitable organizations in Ukraine who work with poor, disabled, and foster children—providing relief with dignity and accountability.
